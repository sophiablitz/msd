About

Sophia Blitz
Founder. Owner. Instructor.

Sophia's teaching style evolved around the core value of respect-- for oneself, one's partner, and the community around. Whether teaching a class or private lesson, she helps her students discover how to respect and connect with themselves, their partner, and the music. Even Sophia's beginner classes are taught with advanced explanations so that students can learn the underlying principles of how to lead and follow effectively.

Sophia's pedagogy is the product of 7+ years of regular social dancing (e.g. Salsa, Bachata, and Zouk), 5+ years of competitive ballroom dancing, and a number of instructor training programs in Salsa, Bachata, and Zouk. Sophia's approach to teaching dance emphasizes the experience of the dancers and dynamic nature of social dancing with a partner. By building the acuity of your senses, you learn to listen and communicate more clearly and more kindly with your partner.

Recognizing that the fundamentals of partner dancing can apply beyond the dance floor, Sophia layers life lessons into her dance classes. Many of her students have found that taking dance classes with Sophia can offer lessons in much more than just dance. What can an improved connection do for your experience of dancing? What about for your experience of life?

Connection. Dance for it. Live for it.
